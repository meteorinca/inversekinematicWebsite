My Mission

Learn by Doing

STEM is not a lecture—it is a workshop. I design robotics kits, coding labs, and digital experiments that let students touch the math and physics behind the world.

Build a Tribe of Makers

From Texas to Seattle, I'm forming a community of curious minds who learn by building, failing, fixing, and laughing together.

Make Engineering Human

STEM is a story about creativity and courage. I help learners see themselves not as test-takers but as inventors shaping humanity's space-faring future.

As a passionate STEM educator, I believe that science, technology, engineering, and mathematics are not just subjects to study—they're tools for understanding and improving our world.

Through workshops, curriculum development, and community outreach, I strive to break down barriers and show that everyone has the potential to be a scientist, engineer, or innovator.
